Rental Requests

Review and approve or deny pending instrument rental requests. Pending requests appear at the top.

The Patreon columns are populated automatically when a request is submitted. Use Refresh Patreon status to re-check all requests against the current Patreon member list — useful if a member recently pledged or their payment lapsed. Note: Patreon lookup matches on email address — the member's Patreon account must use the same email as their member profile for validation to work.

A renter is eligible for a nag email if they meet one or both of these conditions: attendance inactive (no rehearsal attendance within the configured window, or marked inactive) or Patreon inactive (Patreon status is not confirmed active). The nag cooldown prevents sending more than one email per renter within the cooldown period — the Nag button is disabled while a renter is in cooldown. Use Nag all eligible renters to send in bulk to everyone currently eligible.
